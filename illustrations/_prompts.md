## Shared Register Block (appended to every prompt)

> Thin clean vector outlines, flat shapes, editorial precision, consulting-grade illustration, generous negative space. Palette: Deep Space Blue #1A3A5C, Crayola Blue #2878E8, Alice Blue #F2F6FB background, Sky Reflection #8AABC8 mid-tone. No orange, no coral, no peach, no warm skin tones, no warm-coloured clothing, no pure black, no pure white. Background must be Alice Blue #F2F6FB, not white. Figures wear slate or cool-grey clothing in Deep Space Blue or Sky Reflection tones only.

## Shared Figure Language (scenes with people)

Figures: calm, composed, minimal facial detail, clean silhouettes. Clothing in Deep Space Blue #1A3A5C or Sky Reflection #8AABC8. No warm tones on skin or garments.
